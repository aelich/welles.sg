AI - Deep Learning

Welles is an innovative analytics startup helping companies of all sizes create data driven solutions for their business problems. These solutions are typically packaged as software applications that receive data at predefined frequency, run machine learning algorithms at back end and trigger actions based on predictions.

Welles works with enterprises in improving customer experience, customer engagement & automation of manual workloads by:

Creation of Analytics Products

with machine learning/NLP/deep learning capabilities ex: recommendation engine, churn analysis, fraud detection, chatbots etc. Such projects are mostly on BOT (Build, Operate & Transfer) model.

Extended Analytics Team

our team of business analysts/data scientists works closely with clients in identifying business problems, doing root cause analysis, creating actionable insights through data analysis & adhoc reporting.

Machine Learning Consulting

where our senior Machine Learning experts work with clients in defining solution framework, the methodology which client’s internal team can implement themselves.

Our team possess decades of experience thanks to it can provide adequate solutions for a variety of challenges that our customers face every day. We ensure that every aspect of our clients capital program or project is properly governed, well-executed and fully supported from beginning to end.
